French Tenses Masterclass

Present Tense

Used for current actions, habits, and general truths: Je parle français.

Passé Composé

Auxiliary + past participle for completed past actions: J'ai terminé.

Most verbs use avoir; movement/reflexive verbs often use être.

Imparfait

Background descriptions, repeated actions, states: Il faisait beau.

Future and Conditional

Future: infinitive stem + endings (je parlerai).

Conditional expresses politeness/hypothetical outcomes (je voudrais).

Subjunctive (advanced)

Used after expressions of doubt, emotion, necessity: Il faut que tu viennes.
