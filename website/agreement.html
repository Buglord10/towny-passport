Agreement Essentials

Noun Gender and Articles

Masculine singular uses le/un, feminine singular uses la/une.

Plural for both genders typically uses les/des.

Adjective Agreement

Adjectives usually agree in gender and number with nouns: un garçon intelligent, une fille intelligente.

Plural generally adds -s: des garçons intelligents.

Past Participle Agreement

With être, participles agree with the subject: Elles sont arrivées.

With avoir, agreement usually happens only when a preceding direct object is present.

Common Agreement Traps

  • Color adjectives from nouns often stay unchanged: des yeux marron.
  • Compound adjectives may agree partially based on usage.
  • Watch silent endings that still count in writing.
