Conjugation Patterns

Regular Verb Families

-er: parler → je parle, nous parlons

-ir: finir → je finis, nous finissons

-re: vendre → je vends, nous vendons

Top Irregular Verbs

Focus early on être, avoir, aller, faire, pouvoir, vouloir, devoir, venir.

These verbs appear in many tense constructions and exam tasks.

Stem-Changing Verbs

Some verbs shift spelling/pronunciation in certain persons:

  • acheter: j’achète / nous achetons
  • préférer: je préfère / nous préférons
  • manger: nous mangeons (extra e)

Conjugation Study Method

  1. Memorize pronouns and tense endings.
  2. Learn one tense per week with high-frequency verbs.
  3. Write and speak mini-sentences instead of isolated tables.
  4. Review errors and classify: ending, stem, auxiliary, agreement.
